How Cricket Impacts My Life

My name is K Pradeep Kumar, a 21-year-old student living in Andipatti.

Like many students, my days are filled with classes, assignments, exams, and future worries.

But cricket changes everything for me.

Whenever India plays, I forget all my stress. Watching my country play fills me with pride and energy.

During the IPL, my heart beats for RCB. Win or lose, I never miss a match.

Cricket teaches me discipline, patience, teamwork, and never giving up.

Cricket does not just entertain me. It inspires me to become a better person every day.
