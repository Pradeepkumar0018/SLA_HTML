How Cricket Impacts My Life

My name is K Pradeep Kumar, a 21-year-old student living in Andipatti. Like many students, my days are filled with classes, assignments, exams, and future worries. Sometimes, the pressure feels heavy and the mind feels tired.

But cricket changes everything for me.

Whenever India plays, I forget all my stress. I sit in front of the TV with full excitement, cheering every boundary and every wicket. Watching my country play fills me with pride and energy.

During the IPL, my heart beats for RCB. Win or lose, I never miss a match. Supporting RCB teaches me loyalty, hope, and belief.

Cricket is not just a game in my life. It is my inspiration.

When I feel low about studies or life, I remember how cricketers stay patient, practice hard, and never give up. That motivates me to work harder in my own life as a student.

Every evening, playing cricket with friends in my street refreshes my mind. It keeps me active, happy, and connected with others.

Cricket teaches me discipline, patience, teamwork, and never giving up.

Cricket does not just entertain me. It inspires me to become a better person every day.
