How Cricket Impacts My Life My name is K Pradeep Kumar, a 21-year-old student living in Andipatti. Like many students, my days are filled with classes, assignments, exams, and future worries. Sometimes, the pressure feels heavy and the mind feels tired. But cricket changes everything for me. Whenever India national cricket team plays, I forget all my stress. I sit in front of the TV with full excitement, cheering every boundary and every wicket. Watching my country play fills me with pride and energy. During the Indian Premier League, my heart beats for Royal Challengers Bangalore. Win or lose, I never miss a match. Supporting RCB teaches me loyalty, hope, and belief—because even after tough seasons, fans always stand strong. Cricket is not just a game in my life. It is my inspiration. When I feel low about studies or life, I remember how cricketers stay patient, practice hard, and never give up. That motivates me to work harder in my own life as a student. Just like in cricket, I understand that success needs practice, focus, and determination. Every evening, playing cricket with friends in my street refreshes my mind. It keeps me active, happy, and connected with others. Those moments of laughter and teamwork are the best part of my day. Cricket teaches me: Discipline Patience Teamwork Never giving up Cricket does not just entertain me. It inspires me to become a better person every day.
