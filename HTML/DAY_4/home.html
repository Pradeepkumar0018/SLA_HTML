Welcome to TN Election Result 2026

The Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026 created a major political change in the state. TVK led by actor Vijay became the single largest party by winning around 108 seats.

However, no party crossed the majority mark of 118 seats, leading to a hung assembly in Tamil Nadu.

About Tamil Nadu Election 2026

Tamil Nadu Election 2026 is one of the most important democratic events in the state. People from all districts participated actively in voting to elect their representatives.

The election focused on employment, education, infrastructure, agriculture welfare, youth development, and public welfare schemes.
