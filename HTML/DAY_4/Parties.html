Major Political Parties in Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu has several important political parties that play a major role in state elections and government formation.

During the 2026 election, parties conducted campaigns, rallies, and public meetings across Tamil Nadu.

Political Parties

  1. Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (DMK)

    2. DMK focuses on social justice, education, welfare schemes, and infrastructure development.

  2. All India Anna Dravida Munnetra Kazhagam (AIADMK)

    3. AIADMK is known for welfare programs, women support schemes, and rural development.

  3. Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK)

    4. TVK led by actor Vijay became the single largest party in the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election.

  4. Naam Tamilar Katchi (NTK)

    5. NTK focuses on Tamil identity, agriculture, and youth empowerment.
