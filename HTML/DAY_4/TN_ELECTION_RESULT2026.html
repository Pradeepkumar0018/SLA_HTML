Welcome to TN Election Result 2026

The Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026 created a major political change in the state. Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK), led by actor Vijay, became the single largest party by winning around 108 seats in the assembly election.

However, no party crossed the majority mark of 118 seats, leading to a hung assembly. Political parties started alliance discussions and negotiations to form the next government in Tamil Nadu.

About Tamil Nadu Election 2026

Tamil Nadu Election 2026 is one of the most important democratic events in the state. People from all districts participated actively in voting to select their representatives.

Major political parties conducted election campaigns, public meetings, rallies, and awareness programs across Tamil Nadu.

The election focused on important issues such as:

  • Employment Opportunities
  • Education Development
  • Infrastructure Growth
  • Agriculture Welfare
  • Youth Development
  • Women Safety
  • Public Welfare Schemes

Young voters also played an important role in shaping the election result.
