Tamil Nadu Election Dates 2026

The Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026 followed an important election schedule announced by the Election Commission.

Political parties started campaigning after the official election dates were released.

Election Schedule

Event Date
Election Announcement March 1, 2026
Nomination Start Date March 5, 2026
Nomination Last Date March 12, 2026
Campaign Ending Date April 3, 2026
Voting Date April 5, 2026
Result Date April 10, 2026

Important Information

  • Voting was conducted across all districts in Tamil Nadu.
  • Security arrangements were made at polling booths.
  • Electronic Voting Machines (EVM) were used.
  • Young voters actively participated in the election.
  • Election counting started early morning on result day.
